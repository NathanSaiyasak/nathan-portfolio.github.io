I was responsible for end-to-end packaging design for Lydus medical gloves, which are now exported to the US and EU. My work included visual design, content creation, and ensuring compliance with international regulations. I researched suitable design directions, collaborated with the policy team to finalize key information, and worked with suppliers to prepare the box for production. The result is a professional, export-ready package that clients say stands out on shelves for its clean and distinctive look.
